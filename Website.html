About Graham Sarap

Graham Sarap is a proudly Filipino-made dessert brand that brings comfort and delight in every cup. Inspired by the traditional graham float loved across generations, our product features layers of crushed graham crackers, creamy filling, and sweet toppings like mangoes, chocolate, or other local flavors. Wboothether you're craving a quick treat or sharing it with loved ones, Graham Sarap offers a rich, nostalgic taste of home — anytime, anywhere.

Steps in baking graham

Steps in Baking a Graham Dessert Ingredients (for crust): 1½ cups crushed graham crackers ¼ cup white sugar 6 tbsp melted butter Ingredients (for filling): 2 packs (8 oz each) cream cheese, softened ½ cup white sugar 2 eggs 1 tsp vanilla extract ½ cup sour cream (optional for creaminess)
